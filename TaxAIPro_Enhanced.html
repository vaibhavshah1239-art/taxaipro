📑 ITR Forms

Which ITR Form Do You Need?

Every taxpayer has a different profile. Our AI automatically identifies the right form for you based on your income sources.

ITR-1
Sahaj
Salaried Individuals
  • Salaried employees
  • Pension income
  • One house property
  • Other small income sources
  • Income up to ₹50 Lakhs
📎 Docs: Form 16, Bank Statement, Investment Proofs
ITR-2
Capital Gains & HNI
Multiple Income Sources
  • Capital gains (stocks, property)
  • Multiple house properties
  • Foreign assets or income
  • High net worth individuals
  • No business/profession income
📎 Docs: Capital Gains Statement, Property Details, Foreign Income Proof
ITR-3
Business & Profession
Business Owners & Professionals
  • Business owners & proprietors
  • Doctors, lawyers, architects
  • Freelancers with business income
  • Partnership firm partners
  • Directors in companies
📎 Docs: P&L Statement, Balance Sheet, GST Returns
ITR-4
Sugam
Presumptive Tax Scheme
  • Small business owners (turnover <₹2Cr)
  • Professionals (income <₹50L)
  • Freelancers & consultants
  • Simpler presumptive taxation
  • No detailed books required
📎 Docs: Income records, Bank Statement, TDS Certificate

❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ITR filing with TaxAI Pro.

What is the last date to file ITR for AY 2024-25?
+
The due date for filing ITR for most individuals (without audit) for AY 2024-25 is 31st July 2024. For those requiring audit, the due date is 31st October 2024. Filing before the deadline avoids penalties of ₹5,000 (₹1,000 for income below ₹5L).
What documents do I need to file my ITR?
+
Key documents include: Form 16 (from employer), Form 26AS, AIS (Annual Information Statement), bank statements, investment proofs (80C, 80D), home loan certificate, rent receipts (HRA), and capital gains statements from broker. Our AI helps you identify exactly what's needed based on your profile.
How does the AI detect errors in my tax return?
+
Our AI cross-references your data with Form 26AS, AIS, and TIS from the Income Tax portal. It checks for TDS mismatches, missing income declarations, incorrect PAN details, and incorrect deduction claims — preventing tax notices before they happen.
When will I receive my tax refund?
+
Refunds are typically processed within 4-8 weeks of ITR filing verification (e-verification via Aadhaar OTP takes 2 minutes). We track your refund status and notify you at every stage via WhatsApp and email.
Is my financial data safe on TaxAI Pro?
+
Absolutely. We use 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transfers, and your documents are stored in encrypted cloud servers. We never share your data with third parties. Our platform is compliant with Indian data protection standards and you can request data deletion anytime.
I'm a freelancer with multiple clients. Which ITR form should I file?
+
Most freelancers and consultants file ITR-3 or ITR-4 (Sugam under presumptive scheme). If your professional income is below ₹50 Lakhs and you opt for Section 44ADA, ITR-4 is simpler and doesn't require detailed books. Our AI recommends the best form for your exact situation.
What is the difference between Old and New Tax Regime?
+
The Old Regime offers multiple deductions (80C, 80D, HRA, etc.) with higher tax slabs. The New Regime offers lower tax rates but no deductions. Our AI calculator automatically computes your liability under both regimes and recommends which saves you more money based on your specific deductions.
